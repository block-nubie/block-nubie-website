What is BlockNubie?

Blocknubie is a decentralised Ecosystem for Blockchain Startups

Blocknubie aims to simplify and automate the start-up process from ideation through to customer acquisition. Blocknubie will facilitate onboarding of traditional entrepreneurship onto the blockchain

The platform guides end-users and blockchain startups towards setting up your business quickly and effectively, complemented with a number of Dapps to enhance success, structure and speed of the process.

A marketplace for existing blockchain enterprises to provide their services to the BSUC (Blockchain Startup Community) will further enhance community engagement within the Blocknubie economy.

Blocknubie will focus on meeting the full range of blockchain entrepreneurial needs to include capital and investment assistance, entrepreneurship education, networking opportunities, as well as on building an environment that supports investment in blockchain startups.
